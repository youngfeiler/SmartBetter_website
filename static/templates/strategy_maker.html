Live Betting

This setting determines how long (in minutes) after a game starts that your model will stop recommending bets. It lets you customize for pre-game or live betting.

For example, setting this feature to 0 means that you'll only get bets before the game starts.

Setting this feature to 150 will provide picks throughout the entire game. MLB games are averaging 150 minutes this season, so this value enables your model to recommend live-bets throughout the full duration of a game.
