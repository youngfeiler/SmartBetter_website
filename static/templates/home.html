Key Features

AI-Powered Predictions

We built the software that makes it as easy as possible for you to create tailor made sports betting strategies, leveraging machine learning models and historical data.

The ML model is trained on a large data set to produce custom made, AI- optimized +EV betting systems. Sharp bookmaker odds are analyzed to determine the baseline probabilistic market price. Discrepancies between the baseline odds, and available odds on various sports books are quantified to identify scenarios where odds deviate favorably from the fair market price. This represents a +EV edge.

We are the operating system for you to create, backtest, and run your AI-optimized +EV betting strategy.

Comprehensive Data Analysis

By training on a large dataset of past MLB games, the ML model identifies +EV bets and begins recognizing profitable betting patterns; it then optimizes strategies within user criteria to maximize profitability and win-rate.

With just a few clicks, you will be able to create your own AI-optimized +EV betting strategies. Produce historical P&L backtests, track live bets, and be notified of bets in real-time through text and on your personal dashboard
